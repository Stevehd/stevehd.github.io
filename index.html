WHO OR WHAT IS TWIN SQUID?

Twin Squid is an independent games studio that was formed in 2016 by Stephen Harrison-Daly (@steveOHD). Stephen spent 7 years working with the lovely folks in R&D at Sony Computer Entertainment Europe, before leaving it all behind with the plan to create some of the greatest games in the ENTIRE SOLAR SYSTEM. The goal is to create polished games that feel great, and are just good old fashioned fun.
